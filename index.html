About Dr. Dasarathan International School

Dr. Dasarathan International School was established in June 2010 to provide holistic education initially to students of the catchment area surrounding Jangamanaickenpalayam and neighbouring panchayat union communities.

The school has since expanded at a remarkable pace, now accommodating students from all corners of Coimbatore, neighbouring districts, and even overseas — a testament to the trust families place in the Dr. DIS promise of quality education.

  • Research-based curriculum with emphasis on literacy and critical thinking
  • Holistic education with beautiful and sweeping facilities
  • Joyful learning that prepares students for real-world challenges
  • Creative manifestation through arts, music, and performing arts

Academic Framework

Research-Based Curriculum

Dr. DIS follows a curriculum that places a strong emphasis on foundational literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking — equipping students to confidently engage with complex challenges in an ever-changing world.

The academic programme is enriched with opportunities for creative expression, scientific enquiry, and leadership development — going well beyond rote learning to cultivate genuine intellectual curiosity.

  • Strong literacy & numeracy foundation from Early Years
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving integrated across all subjects
  • Science and Mind Box lab-based practical learning
  • Leadership opportunities and project-based assignments
  • Regular academic circulars and progress assessments
  • Structured examination dates communicated to parents in advance
📖
Literacy & Language

Comprehensive English literacy programme with structured reading, writing, and communication skills developed from Pre-KG through Grade 12.

➕
Mathematics

Conceptual mathematics teaching with real-world applications, mental maths, and structured problem-solving from foundational to advanced levels.

🔬
Science & Technology

Lab-based science education covering Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer Science — practical, project-led, and inquiry-driven.

🌏
Social Studies

History, Geography, Civics, and Economics taught through an analytical lens, developing informed, responsible young citizens.

🎨
Arts & Creative Learning

Visual arts, music, and craft integrated into the curriculum to develop creativity, aesthetic sensibility, and emotional intelligence.

💻
Digital & Mind Box Learning

Technology literacy, coding foundations, and innovation projects through the Mind Box Lab — preparing students for a digital future.

How to Apply

Join the Dr. DIS Family

We hope your campus visit will confirm that Dr. Dasarathan International School is the best fit for your child. Applications are welcome for Pre-KG through Grade 11.

1
Visit Campus or Download the Form

Visit Dr. DIS at Jangamanaickenpalayam to collect the prospectus, or download the admission form from this page.

2
Complete & Submit Application

Fill in the application form with your child's previous school records, birth certificate, and two passport photographs.

3
Interaction with Academic Team

A warm, informal meeting with our team helps us understand your child's learning style, interests, and readiness.

4
Enrolment Confirmation

Receive your offer and complete enrolment by submitting the signed form and registration fee. Welcome to Dr. DIS!

⚠️ Admissions are open for 2025–2026. Seats are limited — apply early to secure your child's place.
